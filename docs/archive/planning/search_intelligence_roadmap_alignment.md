# Search Intelligence Roadmap Alignment

## Purpose

This document records the roadmap transition from a source-centric mindset to a market-intelligence mindset.

## Historical Focus

- More sources
- More connectors
- More jobs

## Current Focus

- Better market understanding
- Better discovery coverage
- Better vocabulary coverage
- Better capability-gap visibility

## Key Metrics

- New Companies Discovered
- New Vocabulary Discovered
- Confirmed Origin Jobs
- Exploration Source Contribution
- Search-Term Portfolio Growth

## Expected Long-Term Outcome

A continuously improving understanding of the target market rather than a simple increase in collected job volume.
