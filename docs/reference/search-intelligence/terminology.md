# Search Intelligence Terminology

This document defines the current terminology used by the Search Intelligence subsystem.

## KEEP

The following terms remain active and preferred:

- Bronze Layer
- Silver Layer
- Gold Layer
- Market Evidence
- Company Vocabulary
- Candidate Intelligence
- Search-Term Value
- Capability Gap
- Ground Truth
- Source Type
- Source Role

---

## REDEFINE

### Discovery

Discovery must be qualified.

Preferred:

- Company Discovery
- Market Discovery
- Vocabulary Discovery

Avoid using plain 'Discovery' when a more specific term exists.

---

### Source Value

Source Value is role-dependent.

Discovery-oriented roles:

- New Companies Discovered
- New Vocabulary Discovered
- Confirmed Origin Jobs

Ground Truth roles:

- Relevant Jobs
- Unique Jobs
- Data Quality
- Operational Stability

---

### Search Term

Differentiate between:

#### Search Profile Term

Actively configured search term used for acquisition.

#### Observed Vocabulary

Market terminology learned from evidence and vocabulary analysis.

---

## DEPRECATE

### False Negative

Historical terminology.

Current interpretation:

- Market Discovery Gap
- Coverage Gap
- Missing Market Evidence

The term remains valid historically but should not be used for new architecture decisions.

---

### Source Expansion

Historical terminology.

Preferred wording:

- Discovery Coverage Expansion
- Market Coverage Expansion
